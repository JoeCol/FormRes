Call for Papers
We invite you to submit your up-to-date work in the area of prospective responsibility to the Logic of Responsibility Workshop, to be held in Manchester from the DATES HERE.

All submissions will be reviews and evaluated based on their relevance to the workshop and their clarity. Papers submitted will not be published. Successful submissions will be presented at the workshop. There will be two accepted papers, one with a focus on philosophy and another with a computer science focus. For the accepted papers, one of the authors will be required to present the work at the workshop.

  • Modelling responsibility in Agents
  • Using responsibilities in decision making
  • Using responsibilities in a multi-agent setting
